About Us

~ IISI Mission Statement ~

The young mind holds great power to transform this world into a better place. IISI Youth Leaders work toward enhancing religious understanding and promoting peaceful coexistence, glorifying humans as members of One Human Family by accepting and appreciating our differences and cherishing our commonalities. We strive to promote harmony among all people regardless of faith or culture and are deeply committed to "living our faiths through service" helping those in need. We are all brothers and sisters in the Family of Humanity.

  • Promote Interfaith harmony through dialogues
  • Beholding the essence of UN Sustainable Development Goals through our projects.
  • Organising Interfaith Workshops
  • Interfaith Educational Trips
  • Food & Clothes Distribution
